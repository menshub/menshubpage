Lead The Leaders

The flagship event of the first phase of SHE was 'Lead the Leaders'. The two day residential camp for selected 30 SHE senior members. The programme aimed to inculcate the much needed skills for professional excellence as well as personal competence. The session included a series of talks of experts from various domin. Scheduled with games, discussions and interactive sessions, mentors of SHE could comprehend, what exactly the girls want to become equipped to be a leader by all means. Ultimately, the program sought to mentor the attendees to become leaders for SHE, who would go on to mould the junior members.

SHE Coders

The 'SHE Coders' platform was initiated to provide an online platform for students to develop their coding skills with alumnae as mentors. The program provided flexible timing for conducting activities which were designed in such a way so as to familiarize students with industry trends. The first batch of SHE Coders had 20 active participants, who engaged in self and peer-based learning to implement React framework for web development. Training on Matlab was also provided with similar intent, and was concluded with 30 contact hours.

Resume Building Workshop

A workshop on Resume Building was conducted in association with the Rethink Foundation. Through the workshop, it was intended to introduce participants to the nuances of resume etiquette. The day long programme worked towards instilling good resume writing skills, with a focus on exploring job descriptions for each persons dream job while still in college, so as to cultivate the relevant industry skills and create resumes that represent them better.

SHE Coders-2

After the successful launch of SHE Coders, a group of students with no prior experience in coding approached with the wish to learn coding. SHE Coders 2 was launched after the first phase to extend it to a bigger crowd, from first year students. It was under the leadership of first SHE coders, that more students with least experience of coding were included.
